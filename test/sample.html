ATTENTION SENIOR STUDENTS: Are you interested in studying food systems, biology, global food markets, human health, food, nutrition, animals, plants, the environment, or sustainability after high school? If so, consider attending the University of British Columbia’s Land and Food Systems Fall Preview Day! It will take place on Saturday November 24th from 10:00 am to 12:30 pm at the UBC Vancouver campus. At the event you will learn about the degree programs offered through the faculty of Land and Food systems and have your application questions answered.

If interested sign up online at: https://ubc.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_0Cfy6boe3uwpbdH

Or see your counsellor. Thanks! Ms. Bekkering
